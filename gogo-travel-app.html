Gogo Travel App

CASE STUDY, MOBILE APP, UX DESIGN, UX RESEARCH

The high level scope of this project was to design an app for International travellers. The role is to assist travellers from planning to booking and even assist in their local experience through customer suggestions and notifications at right time. Also the app provide extra service (more information below) to their users and also interact with them in each and every stage throuhout the trip. The mobile app delivers an innovative and consistent experience to international travellers in a single platform.

M Y R O L E : UX Designer    T O O L S : Figma, Miro, Adobe Illustrator

CASE STUDY, MOBILE APP, UX DESIGN, UX RESEARCH

THE PROBLEM

Most of the travel apps do not consider the fact that travellers want to experience there trip in the real essence rather that just completing a task. So, in the present situation international travellers need to rely on multiple apps and google from planning to booking to complete their trip. Travellers are frustrated by organising the trip rather than experiencing it.

I conducted primary and secondary user research to better understand this problem space.

WHAT WE LEARNED

Planning is the biggest barrier for travellers   |   Switching between multiple Apps is a tiring process   |   Getting local experience is always a challenge There is currently no reason for loyalty in the travel space   |   Users are looking for customised solotuions

I conducted primary and secondary user research to better understand this problem space.

“I hve to compare the price and deals in 10 different sites and then make my booking”

“I want an app that truly understand what I want and only give me what I want and not what they think I might like”

“Even after surffing through multiple platforms its a time cunsuming process to plan a proper trip”

THE SOLUTION

To build a solution to international travellers from planning to booking on their multiple needs including travel, stay, activities, events, local commute, shopping and dinning. Based on the insights that I got from the primary and secondary user research my goal was to rapidly design a single platform which provide experience that was simple, relevant and trustworthy. It can save time and provide the best choices. Eventually the trip experience will be better, hence he/she doesn’t have to search between apps.

an innovative solutions to user pain points I decide to create a personalized mobile app called ‘GOGO’

To gather qualitative in-depth insights about the traveller opinion on their trip planning phase and their stay at destination. We decided to interview five potential travellers across the demographic as well as different age group ranging between 28 -50 age group.

Quantitative: - Online Survey

After gathering qualitative results from the above interviews, as a secondary user research study to determine what an international and domestic traveller might look for planning his trip and why and his destination. ONLINE SURVEY INSIGHTS

Behaviour

After successfully running the survey we got 36 completed responses from the age group between 25 and 48. All of them are travellers and most of them are international travellers and often make dramatic trips and one international trip per year.

Behaviour

  • • Most of them are family and leisure travellers
  • • Their primary intention is to rest and have a relaxed time
  • • Most of them depend on Google to plan the trips and use multiple travel apps
  • • Above 50% of the people are looking for ease of use.
  • • Almost everyone depends on their travel app.
  • • Choice of activities are through Google search, word of mouth and through travel apps
  • • Prefer fun and cultural activities most of the times
  • • Most of them are flexible to explore new events if they get a better option.
  • • There is 50:50 chance to influence the trip decision
  • • PNs and marketing communications influence their trip decision
  • • All of them are budget travellers
  • • Most of them are rated their trip above average
